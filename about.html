About FedCore

Backed by a team of business professionals with over 7 years of combined experience in the federal contracting space.

Our Story

FedCore LLC is an emerging government contracting firm specializing in retail supply and consumable goods, delivering reliable, high quality products and services. We understand the core of government procurement; how to navigate requirements, align with agency standards, and effectively fulfill mission critical needs with accuracy and efficiency.

With a focus on responsiveness, compliance, and exceptional customer service, we are committed to building lasting partnerships and exceeding expectations across every engagement.
